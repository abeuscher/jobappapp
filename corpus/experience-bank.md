# Experience bank

Each chapter/role with multiple bullet variants emphasizing different angles
(leadership, technical depth, outcomes). The generation layer may only assert
claims that appear here — write bullets you can stand behind verbatim.

## Example: Founder / Technical Lead, Acme Co (2019–2024)

### Leadership angle
- Built and led a team of N engineers from first hire through product launch.

### Technical depth angle
- Designed and shipped the core X system in TypeScript/Node handling Y requests/day.

### Outcomes angle
- Grew revenue/users from A to B over C months.
